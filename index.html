Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There will be four crystals displayed as buttons on the page. The value of each crystals is hidden from you until you click on it. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game if your total score matches the random number, you lose if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
